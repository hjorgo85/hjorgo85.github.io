## Our Commitment to Transparency

Sea Life Planet is a participant in the **Amazon Services LLC Associates Program**, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com and affiliated sites.

In plain terms: when you click certain links on this Site — usually marked with a "Check Price on Amazon" button — and go on to make a purchase, **we may earn a small commission**. This comes at **no additional cost to you**; the price you pay is the same whether or not you use our link.

## How This Affects Our Recommendations

Affiliate income helps fund the research, testing, and writing that goes into our guides. It does **not** change what we recommend:

- We only link to products we've used, researched, or would genuinely recommend to a fellow fishkeeper.
- We do not accept payment from manufacturers in exchange for positive reviews.
- We do not display live pricing on this Site (prices change frequently on Amazon) — always confirm current price and availability on the retailer's page before purchasing.

## Where You'll See Affiliate Links

Affiliate links typically appear in:

- Product review articles (e.g. our [Product Reviews](/product-reviews/) section)
- Equipment recommendations within species care guides (filters, heaters, lighting, food, etc.)

Each affiliate product mention includes a visible disclosure near the link itself, in addition to this page.

## We'd Love to Hear From You

Whether you've got a question about a care guide, spotted something that needs correcting, or want to suggest a topic for us to cover, here's how to reach the Sea Life Planet team.

### General Questions & Corrections

For species care questions, content corrections, or general feedback, email us at:

**[hello@sealifeplanet.com](mailto:hello@sealifeplanet.com)**

We aim to respond within 2-3 business days. If you're reporting an error in a guide, please include the page URL and a brief description — corrections are reviewed by our team before publishing.

### Partnerships & Press

For collaboration, product review, or media inquiries, email:

**[partnerships@sealifeplanet.com](mailto:partnerships@sealifeplanet.com)**

### Follow Along

You can also find us on the social channels linked in the footer below for tank updates, species spotlights, and behind-the-scenes content.

## Before You Write In

If your question is about a specific fish species or tank setup issue, check whether we already have a guide covering it — browse [Saltwater Fishkeeping](/saltwater/), [Freshwater Fish](/freshwater-fish/), or [all fish species guides](/fish-types/). Many common questions (tank size, tank mates, water parameters) are answered directly in each species profile's FAQ section.

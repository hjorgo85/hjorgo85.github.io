Fish Species Care Guides: Freshwater & Saltwater

Every species guide on Sea Life Planet follows the same structure: realistic tank size and water parameter requirements, diet, tank mate compatibility, and answers to the questions that actually come up once you own the fish. Browse by category below, or use the Saltwater and Freshwater Fish sections for category-level guidance.

How to Use This Page

This page is the master index of every species profile on the site. Each guide includes a Quick Facts box (scientific name, tank size, temperament, diet, and more) near the top, followed by detailed sections on tank setup, water parameters, feeding, compatibility, and — where relevant — breeding behavior.

If you're choosing a species for a specific tank size or setup, start with the Quick Facts box on each guide to quickly rule fish in or out before reading the full profile.

This Privacy Policy explains how Sea Life Planet ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit sealifeplanet.com (the "Site").

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information

Like most websites, we use analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) that automatically collect certain information about your visit, including:

- Pages viewed and time spent on the Site
- Approximate location (city/country level, based on IP address)
- Browser type, device type, and operating system
- Referring website or search engine

This information is collected in aggregate and is not used to personally identify you.

### Information You Provide

If you contact us by email, we collect the information you choose to include in that message (such as your name and email address) solely to respond to your inquiry.

## Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

- Understand how visitors use the Site (analytics)
- Remember your preferences
- Support affiliate link tracking (see below)

You can disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some Site functionality.

## Children's Privacy

This Site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

You may opt out of personalized analytics tracking by adjusting your browser's cookie settings or using browser extensions that block tracking scripts.
